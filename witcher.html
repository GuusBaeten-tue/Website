The gist of this is a monster of the week series.
The party form a group (Coven) of monster hunters, so called "Witchers". Every class is a Witcher, their level ups are flavored as mutations they can choose.

Learning a monsters weakness and exploiting it in follow up encounters is half the fun. Oils, potions, and decoctions should be available to the players
